Whole Life Insurance with Guaranteed Income

ЁЯУШ Plan Summary тАУ English

LIC Jeevan Umang is a participating, non-linked whole life insurance plan that provides annual survival income along with life-long protection up to 100 years.

  • Policy term: Up to age 100 years
  • Premium paying term: Limited (shorter than policy term)
  • Survival benefit: 8% of Basic Sum Assured every year after PPT
  • Maturity benefit: Lump sum at age 100
  • Death benefit: Sum Assured + bonuses
  • Loan facility available
  • Minimum Sum Assured: тВ╣2,00,000
