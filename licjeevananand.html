ЁЯУШ Plan Summary тАУ English

LIC New Jeevan Anand is a non-linked, participating life insurance plan that combines savings and lifelong protection. The policy offers financial protection throughout life even after the maturity benefit is paid.

  • Maturity Benefit: Sum Assured + Bonuses (Reversionary + Final Additional).
  • Life Long Cover: After maturity, the life cover (Basic Sum Assured) continues for life for free.
  • Eligibility: Entry age 18тАУ50 years; Max maturity age 75 years.
  • Policy Term: 15тАУ35 years.
  • Minimum Sum Assured: тВ╣1,00,000.
  • Loan Facility: Available after 2 years of premium payment.
